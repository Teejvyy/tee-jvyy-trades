The Trading Journey of Victoria Duke

About Tori Trades

With nine years of experience in the trading world, Victoria Duke—known as "Tori Trades"—offers a wealth of insights and knowledge that few can match. Starting her journey in 2015 with the guidance of her uncle, Tori’s passion for trading grew over years of dedication, perseverance, and continuous learning. Her goal is not only to master the market but also to help others find confidence in their own trading journeys.


Transparent Approach

Tori is known for her transparent approach to trading. She believes that honesty and clarity are essential for anyone venturing into the trading world, ensuring her community always understands the rationale behind each trading decision. This openness fosters trust and keeps traders informed at every step.

Down-to-Earth Teaching Style

One of Tori's standout qualities is her down-to-earth teaching style. She knows that trading concepts can be complex and is committed to making them accessible, using relatable examples and clear communication. Her goal is for every trader to feel confident and capable, no matter their experience level.

Embracing Trading Realities

While many focus solely on trading's rewards, Tori embraces the challenges and ups and downs of the market. She prepares her students for the full picture, emphasizing resilience and realistic expectations. This honest perspective has earned her a loyal following of traders who appreciate her genuine approach.

Comprehensive Learning and Support

In Tori’s program, each trader receives a customized plan tailored to their strengths and goals. Her streamlined system is designed for sustained growth, and she’s built a supportive community where traders can feel comfortable and confident when making decisions. Together, they create an encouraging environment where learning and growth flourish.

Early Beginnings

Tori’s journey began in 2015 when her uncle introduced her and her sister to trading. From early morning discussions over coffee to hours of training, Tori immersed herself in learning. What started as a family endeavor has blossomed into a career, with Tori now a seasoned student of the markets, continually honing her skills and sharing her knowledge with others.
